Banana Pancakes

Indulge in the blissful simplicity of banana pancakesâ€”a delightful twist to the classic breakfast favorite. Mashed ripe bananas seamlessly blend into a fluffy batter, creating a symphony of sweet, golden perfection. With a hint of vanilla and a drizzle of maple syrup, each bite unveils a harmonious fusion of flavors. Elevate your morning ritual with these easy-to-make, delectable pancakes, promising a comforting and delicious start to your day.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Steak Breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, mashed bananas, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

step 2

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are okay. Let the batter rest for a few minutes. Preheat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.

step 3

Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown Stack the pancakes on a plate and serve with maple syrup and sliced bananas and ENJOY!!

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 cup buttermilk

1 large egg

2 ripe bananas, mashed

2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Maple syrup and sliced bananas for serving

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid
